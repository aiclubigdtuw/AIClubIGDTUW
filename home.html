Dr. Amita Dev

Honourable Vice Chancellor, Patron - AI CLUB

Her journey with IGDTUW, since its inception in 1998 as IGIT, has been exemplary, culminating in the transformation of this institution into an icon for women empowerment. Under her leadership, IGDTUW has evolved into a national flagship, research-led university, with a mission to create an environment for excellence in professional education and ensure active participation in the fields of Engineering, Science, Management, and Technology. She aims to strike a fine balance between the world of work and life, and considers her students as mascots of this mission.

Dr. Arun Sharma

Faculty Coordinator - AI CLUB

Prof. Arun Sharma is a revered academic at IGDTUW, with over two and a half decades of pedagogical expertise, complemented by a doctoral degree. His proficiency extends across the domains of Machine Learning, Data Science and Software Engineering, marked by a prodigious portfolio of 75+ scholarly publications and successful mentorship of 10 PhD scholars.His unwavering dedication to empowering students and fostering innovation establishes him as a beacon of inspiration in the academic realm.
